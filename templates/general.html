General Practice

The General Practice Department continues to be the Nerve Center of the VCARE. Individual experiences range from 5-30 years, some with additional skills in Surgery, ENT, Pain Management, Anesthesia, Pulmonology, OB/GYN, Infection Control & Health Promotion, Kidney Dialysis, Leprosy Care, Anti-Smoking Campaign, Community Clinics, Counseling, Leadership and Management. The GPs run the Urgent Care Unit, attend on out-patients and also admit in-patients to the ward if necessary. Our members stretch from the Far East to the United Kingdom, speaking English, Arabic, Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Malayalam, Urdu etc.
